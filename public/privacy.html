Last Updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

At Vinyl Analog, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our audio transformation services.

Information Collection

We collect email addresses only from users who choose to create an account for unlimited playback. We also collect anonymous usage data, such as which YouTube genres are processed, to improve our "Vinyl Engine" logic.

Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies to remember your theme preferences and to facilitate Google AdSense. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites on the Internet.

Third-Party Data

We do not sell your personal email information to third parties. Data regarding listening habits is aggregated anonymously for analytical purposes related to audio trends.
